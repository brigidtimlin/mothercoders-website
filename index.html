Hello

Hi, I'm Brigid. I'm a researcher and evaluator at the New York City Department of Education where I study the impact of a program on educators and thier instructional practices. I came to education research via book publishing. Between careers I took a break from the paid workforce to be with my family. Education research has been a wonderful place for me to combine my love of reading and stories with my commitment to improving education systems for every student. I have the opportunity to work with qualitative data daily; this site is a place for me to practice and share data analysis, visualization, and data storytelling projects. Thank you for stopping by!
